Rank the Value/Future of Stellar Tokens by Crowd Wisdom

Ranking

(Goal: a lightweight & trustless CoinMarketCap. TODO: automatic sort by num; integrate into MagicWallet; cover other chains)
Last updated just now
By providing the ranking utility, we have expanded the scope of MVote from a pure voting-based reward token to a more generic voting token for many other areas.
As the first use case, we request the community to use their crowd wisdom to rank/vote the (investment) value of different tokens on Stellar by burning one or more MVT. This brings three benefits: (1) for new investors to Stellar, they could quickly know which tokens they could invest; (2) for existing investors on Stellar, you can know other people's attitude towards different tokens; and (3) this gives different token teams to advertise their tokens, especially during the initial launch, by burning at least 100 MVT.
Disclaimer: you should NOT treat the ranking result here as investment advice.
